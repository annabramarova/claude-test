About Att-Las

Redefining Pre-Owned Luxury

We're building a marketplace where trust, curation, and discovery come together. Every boutique vetted. Every piece authenticated. Every experience elevated.

Our Story

Att-Las was born from a simple observation: luxury pre-owned fashion deserved better than peer-to-peer marketplaces. We envisioned a platform where discerning buyers could shop with the same confidence and experience they expect from Net-a-Porter or MyTheresa—but for pre-owned pieces.

We partnered exclusively with established independent boutiques—real businesses with proven track records, expertise, and reputations to uphold. No anonymous sellers. No uncertainty. Just trusted boutiques offering authenticated luxury.

Today, Att-Las connects you with a curated network of vetted boutiques across Europe, each bringing their own expertise and carefully selected inventory to our platform.
