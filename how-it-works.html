Common Questions

How are items authenticated?

Each boutique employs certified authentication experts who verify every piece before listing. They examine materials, construction, hardware, stamps, and serial numbers against manufacturer specifications. Authentication certificates are included with every purchase.

What if I need to return an item?

Return policies are set by individual boutiques and clearly stated on each product page. Most boutiques offer returns within 7-14 days if the item is in original condition. Contact the boutique directly to arrange returns.

How does shipping work?

Boutiques handle their own shipping using insured, tracked carriers. Shipping costs and delivery times are shown at checkout. Most European deliveries arrive within 3-5 business days.

Can I contact boutiques directly?

Absolutely! Each boutique profile includes contact information. They're happy to answer questions about specific items, provide additional photos, or discuss their collection.
